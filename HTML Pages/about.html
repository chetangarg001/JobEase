ABOUT US

Welcome to the online Job Portal - JobEase. It provides facility to the Job Seeker to search for various jobs as per his qualification. Here Job Seeker can registered himself on the web portal and create his profile along with his educational information. Job seekers can search various jobs and apply for the Job.

This Portal is also designed for the various employers who require to recruit employees in their organization. Employers can register themselves on this web portal and then can upload information of various job vacancies in their organization. Employeer can view the applications of Job Seeker and send call letter to the job seekers.

JobEase is the most innovative and second largest online job portal in India. Founded in 2008, over the past decade, JobEase has become a prominent name in the recruitment industry. The popularity of the portal is evident from the fact that it has crossed the 3.4 crore candidate landmark and has more than 3 lakh latest job vacancies from leading companies on the site.

JobEase connects jobseekers and recruiters by accurately matching candidate profiles to the relevant job openings through an advanced 2-way matching technology. While most job portals only focus on getting candidates the next job, JobEase focuses on the entire career growth of candidates. To this end, Jobease has launched Shine Learning- India’s largest career skills site for working professionals with over 500+ courses & certifications.

As the industry shifts towards mobile, JobEase.com is leading the transition and is the fastest growing job portal on mobile devices, witnessing a 100% YOY growth in mobile traffic and also offers on-the-go jobs through the JobEase Search App.

JobEase works closely to bridge the gap between talent & opportunities and offers end-to-end recruitment solutions. JobEase Job Fair brings candidates and top employers under one roof. While Shine HR Conclave brings top HR leaders to share insights on latest trends, innovations & best practices in the HR industry. JobEase also has a large reach through its print product, JobEase Jobs – the Tuesday Job supplement of Hindustan Times– making it the only job portal with an integrated print and online offering.
